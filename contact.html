FAQ Section

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Cybvars early access

When will Cybvars be available?

We're launching our beta program in Q2 2025, with full release planned for Q4 2025. Early access members get priority beta invitations.

Is early access free?

Yes! Early access and beta testing are completely free. You'll also receive exclusive pricing when we launch commercially.

What platforms will be supported?

Cybvars will be available on Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, and as browser extensions for Chrome, Firefox, and Safari.

How do I provide feedback?

Early access members get access to our private feedback portal, direct communication with our team, and monthly feedback sessions.

Can I use Cybvars for my business?

We're developing both individual and business plans. Enterprise features will be available during the beta phase.

What about data privacy?

Privacy is our top priority. We use zero-knowledge architecture and never store your personal data. All processing happens locally when possible.
